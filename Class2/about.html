About

Roronoa Zoro, also known as "Pirate Hunter" Zoro, is a main combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates

Sword

Zoro wields three katanas at a time, each with its own significance and grade. His first-ever sword, Wado Ichimonji, is one of the 21 Great Grade Swords. Zoro acquires Sandai Kitetsu, a cursed Grade Sword, and Yubashiri, a Skillful Grade Sword, during his journey with the Straw Hats.
